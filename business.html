Operations involving contacting sponsors, budgeting, allocation of funds, and fundraisers are handled by the business team. The goal of the business team is to acquire all necessary funds as early as possible, so that the project could proceed according to schedule. Potential donors are reached through direct phone calls, emails and/or letters.
