What does this team member to? Keep it short! This is also a great spot for social links!

The Solar Cell team is responsible for the design and layout of the solar array, supplementary array, the charge system, and most importantly the connections to the battery system. By engineering a unique approach to our solar array by utilizing high efficiency solar panels, and an additional solar array to be used exclusively during checkpoints, we generate storable energy. This storable energy is transferred from the array, and to the charge controller, referred to as the Maximum Power Point Tracker. The MPPT will transfer what can potentially be an unsteady power signal, into a very useful very efficient power signal which is then transferred to the batteries for storage. Once it is stored within the batteries, it is our duty to insure the battery team will be able to distribute this generated power to the rest of the vehicle for use.
